## TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS FOR ALL IMAGES:

- **Format**: JPG (high quality, 85-90% compression)
- **Color Profile**: sRGB
- **Resolution**: 72 DPI for web (300 DPI for print materials)
- **Style**: Professional product photography, clean, well-lit
- **Background**: Mostly white or neutral, some lifestyle shots with blurred backgrounds
- **Brand Colors**: Incorporate teal (#0BADA9) as accent color where appropriate
- **Mood**: Professional, elegant, artisanal, trustworthy, premium quality
